I’m a mathematician currently based at the University of Wollongong in Australia. I love geometry and analysis, and have a wide range of interests that combine these two passions. My PhD work was on fourth-order geometric evolution equations. After my PhD, I was awarded an Alexander von Humboldt Research Fellowship to be taken at the Otto von Guericke Universitaet Magdeburg, with Prof. Dr. Hans-Christoph Grunau. After this, I started at the University of Wollongong. I have been awarded an ARC Discovery Project, a biennien Peter Schwerdtfeger award, and an AustMS Early Career Teaching Excellence award.
